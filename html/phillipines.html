The Phillipnes has amazing pristine beaches and crystal clear blue water! Choice beaches to choose from, and delightful Filipino cusine! It is hard to imagine not wanting to go on vacation in such a beautiful set of islands. What are you waiting for? The inviting waters, the salty breeze, the amicable locals, they are missing one thing -- YOU!



There are many great features in The Phillipines, some particularly dangerous like it's proximity to the equator causing frequent earthquakes and typhoons, watch out though! If you can make it throught the "Ring of Fire" as they call it, paradise awaits on the other side! If you're a traveler like myself, the Filipino cuisine should activate your salivary glands. The food is as diverse as their culture, but be sure to try the Sinigang when presented the opportunity! A delicious sour broth, infused with tamarind, vegetables and my choice sinigang na hipon -- Shrimp!
