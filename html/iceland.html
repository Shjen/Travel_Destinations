♨️ Iceland, and it's Fabled Hot Springs! ♨️

Work getting you down? Need a break? Iceland is the perfect destination! Naturalistic thermal features arrayed throughout the island guarantees discovery of a steamy place to relax and ease your everyday stress!
Reykjavík, the capital of Iceland has long been reknown for it's hot springs, the name itself loosely translates to "Smoke Cove" due to the thermal vapors constant presence.


Within the cities the water supplying homes is usually boiling hot and has to be cooled down, instead of heated up! The most popular hot springs is iconic for it's milky blue tinted water -- The Blue Lagoon. Definitely worth taking a mineral-rich dip! The majority of hot springs in Iceland are very natural, what they lack in facilities they more than make up in a beauty only nature can create.
