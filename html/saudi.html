🐪 Saudi Arabia...The Oasis in the Desert! 🐪

Saudi Arabia is a Kingdom which claims most of the Arabian Peninsula. Random fact -- They have one of the youngest populations since 50% of their vast population are under 25 years old! Although their tourism isn't the strongest right now, they have plans to increase their leisure tourism attraction for the first time. Mostly it attracts religious pilgrimages.


Perhaps the mystic effect of having it's borders closed for so long to international travelers makes it quite an exotic destination! The vast deserts give way to an equally impressive city -- an Oasis in the desert. With it's tall iconic buildings, and clear displays of wealth, Saudi Arabia. Especially of note are the Mosques that litter the cities, blending ancient architecture with tall modern buildings in a beautiful synergy.
